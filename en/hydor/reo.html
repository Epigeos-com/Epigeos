Tired of those pesky Chrisitan and Roman norms? Why not use some other calendars?

For real tho, it's just a better system and that opinion fact is totally not biased by my love for ancient Greece.

Info Download

Reo (grc. ῥέω - to flow) helps you track the flow of time in various calendars and with some astronomical info!

So far there's Gregorian and Attic calendars, but who knows what will come later...

The attic calendar is a solar-lunar calendar that actually bases the time on nature.
